How can I order from foodpanda?

To order foodpanda, you simply need to find the restaurants or shops that deliver to your location. Add your address, browse through the available restaurants or shops, choose your order and proceed to checkout for payment. Your order will be delivered in no time!

Choose from dozens of cuisines and order online for home delivery

At foodpanda Cambodia, we make sure you have the best food delivery experience. Offering food ranging from steaks, sushi, fried chicken, Laab, fried noodles, or even donuts, foodpanda has got you covered.

Steps to Order:
  1. Find a restaurant: Enter your address on the homepage. Browse the extensive list of restaurants that deliver to your area.
  2. Choose your dishes: Browse the menu and select dishes you would like to order.
  3. Checkout & Payment: Click "ORDER NOW" and enter your delivery address. Follow the checkout instructions.
  4. Delivery: Receive an email and SMS confirming your order and delivery time.
Does foodpanda deliver 24 hours?

No, foodpanda in Cambodia delivers from 6 am to 12 am midnight. Availability may vary depending on the restaurant or shop.

What restaurants and shops let you order online?

There are hundreds of businesses on foodpanda Cambodia including KFC, Texas Chicken, The Pizza Company, Dairy Queen, Bonchon Chicken, and many more.

What is the difference between delivery and Pick-Up?

If you choose delivery, foodpanda will deliver to your address. For Pick-Up, you self-collect your order from the restaurant.

Is there a minimum amount I need to spend to order foodpanda?

Yes. If your order is less than the minimum order value, you can pay the difference.
